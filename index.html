My Recipe for a great PB&J sandwich

Follow these simple directions to create the best PB&J you've ever had!

You will need:

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • A Knife
  • Plate

Steps:

  1. Place bread on plate
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly on other slice
  4. Put slices together
  5. Cut in half with knife
