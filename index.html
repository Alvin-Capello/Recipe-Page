Cruelty-Free Breakfast

Instead of bacon and eggs, try this simple but filling Vegan breakfast alternative

Here are the ingredients:

  • 1 package of vegan instant oatmeal
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon of cinammon

Follow these steps:

  1. Make vegan oatmeal according to instructions on package
  2. Slice banana into thin slices
  3. Mix banana in with oatmeal
  4. Mix peanut butter in with oatmeal
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon
  6. Stir until the oatmeal is an even consistency
  7. Enjoy!
